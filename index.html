How do you understand fruit juice?

Fruit juice is a method of squeezing out juice from fruits, vegetables, and fruits, but still keeping all the nutrients, including enzymes and vitamins of that food. When juicing, you will remove the fiber, skin of fruits and vegetables that are not good for digestion.

Instead of eating fruit every day, the alternative method of drinking juice will help your body absorb the full nutritional ingredients and correct dosage. Especially, it does not bring the feeling of fullness like when eating a lot of fruit at the same time. Besides, fruit juice can be combined on 2 juiced foods, bringing more great benefits and saving time.
